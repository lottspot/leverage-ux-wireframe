Knowledge is power

Are your elected officials really working for you? It's easy to get drawn in by the ads you see on TV or sprinkled around the city, but it is who pays for these ads that may be a greater predictor of what your elected officials will do once in office than the content of these ads themselves.

Learn where the money comes from that run your elected officials' campaigns. Take back the power to make an informed decision. Make sure the city of Philadelphia is working for you.
